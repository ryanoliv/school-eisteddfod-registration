Reddam House BedfordView Presents

The Concours D’Arts

Hosted annually by Reddam House Bedfordview, this event has become a cultural highlight for Inspired Schools worldwide. The Concours D’Arts is a platform that celebrates young performers, dancers, dramatists, musicians, designers, writers, and artists from all over the world.

Key Dates

Registration Opens: 24 March 2025

Submission Period: 21 April – 25 April 2025

Enter Now Learn More

Our eisteddfod is open to individuals, duos and groups of all sizes in the following categories

Whether you're an individual, duo, or part of a group, there's a category for everyone to shine. Join us in celebrating creativity across various performing arts disciplines!

Languages, Speech & Drama

Express yourself through powerful words and captivating performances! Whether it’s poetry, monologues, storytelling, or drama, this category celebrates the art of communication and performance.

Instrumental & Vocal Music

From solo performances to ensemble pieces, this category welcomes musicians of all skill levels. Show off your talents in classical, jazz, contemporary, or choral music.

Dance

Step onto the stage and let movement tell your story. Whether you specialize in ballet, contemporary, hip-hop, or cultural dances, this category showcases the beauty of motion.

Visual & Art Design

Creativity knows no bounds! Display your artistic talent through painting, digital art, photography, or mixed media. Let your imagination come to life in stunning visual forms.

Participating Schools

Since 2020, more than 40 Inspired schools have participated in this online event, showcasing incredible talent from around the world. Concours D’Arts has become a cultural highlight of the school calendar since 2020 and is open to all Inspired schools globally, as well as independent schools.
